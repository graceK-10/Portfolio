About me

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based in South Africa, with a growing passion for DevSecOps and cloud technologies. While my foundation lies in web development, I also have a keen interest in UI/UX design principles and strive to build digital experiences that are not only functional but also intuitive and engaging.

Currently, I’m expanding my expertise into DevSecOps while pursuing part-time Honours studies in Network and Security Engineering, where I’m deepening my understanding of secure infrastructure, cybersecurity practices, and systems architecture. This academic pursuit complements my professional journey, as I transition further into the world of DevSecOps—integrating security at every phase of the development lifecycle.

My work focuses on creating responsive web platforms, leveraging modern technologies and clean code to solve real-world problems. I also develop mobile applications using no-code tools such as FlutterFlow and Glide—ideal for rapid prototyping and delivering polished MVPs with efficiency. Each project I take on is approached with a strategic mindset, a commitment to quality, and a goal to deliver solutions that reflect the identity and purpose of the people and businesses behind them.
